Morioh University

(杜王町, Moriō-chō)

Morioh University is a fictional higher education institution located in the peaceful coastal town of Morioh, known for its inspiring atmosphere and strong community spirit. As a center for knowledge and innovation, the university is committed to producing graduates who excel academically and embody integrity and social responsibility.


With a team of dedicated educators and modern learning facilities, Morioh University offers a wide range of distinguished programs in science, humanities, information technology, and creative arts. The campus provides a serene and inclusive environment that encourages students to grow, explore, and contribute meaningfully to society. Guided by its motto, "Knowledge, Integrity, and Innovation", Morioh University strives to remain relevant, forward-thinking, and responsive to the challenges of the modern world.

Proudly Morioh. Proudly You. We Love, MORIOH-CHO!
